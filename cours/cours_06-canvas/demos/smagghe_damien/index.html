The tron game

How to play ?

  • You need to be 2
  • Each player will control a Tron bike
  • The tron bike is always moving
  • The goal is to avoid shield left by the other player and yours
  • If you hit a border of the game or a shield you'll lose

keyboard

  • A Player 1 left rotation
  • E Player 1 right rotation
  • ← Player 2 left rotation
  • → Player 2 right rotation
